Prayers

My LORD.


I knew Thee in the wilderness O' Mighty One.


When I wandered in the land of great drought and desolation.


When the sun scorched me and the winds blew with such fury that they seemed to tear apart the very soul of my land.


And it was in that barren place LORD that I sought Thee... with all of my heart, for I knew that Thou Art My Only refuge.


It is there in the wilderness where My spirit was humbled and My Soul was tried.


I found Thee O' LORD, as a shelter in the storm, as a fortress of strength in the midst of weakness, as a stream of Living Eternity in the desert of my Soul.


According to thine pasture, according to the measure of Thy Grace, I am filled, and I am compounded by thy Holy Ghost.


I will be like unto You as a lion, as a mighty beast of the field, strong and fierce, and protector of Thine inheritance.


I will be a Bull Baba wami, with Thy mighty horns to trample down the enemy, and to stand as an obliterator in the land that Thou hast given.


I will be as a leopard by Thy wayside, swift and vigilant, watching over the path that Thou hast set for me.


Ndizoba ibhele baba... elinguKumkani - Yea my LORD, I proclaim - I Am Forever thine Eternally yours.


You my Song of Eternal Praise that resounds throughout All Infinity.


Glory Be unto You, for Thou Art the Eternal One, the beginning and the end, the Alpha and the Omega.


Thy Power is unmatched, Thy Wisdom unparalleled - and Thy Love Is immeasurable.


Thou Art the source of All Life, the Giver of every good and perfect gift, and to Thee alone be all Glory, now and Forevermore.


He - Hima Shima, Mar Shaddai - Lord Hirashima.


Worthy Art Thou, thou art... Worthy Is the King of Glory.
