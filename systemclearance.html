What to Do if Pulled Over

If you're ever pulled over by authorities, please remain calm and follow the steps outlined below to ensure that your rights are protected and you stay informed about the process.

  • Stay Calm: Always stay calm and remain respectful.
  • Provide Your Documentation: Show your driver's license, vehicle registration, and proof of insurance.
  • Know Your Rights: You have the right to remain silent and to ask for clarification on why you were stopped.
  • Don't Engage in Arguments: If you feel your rights are being violated, remain respectful and note down the details for later.
  • Request Legal Assistance: If necessary, request a lawyer or legal representative to be present during the interaction.
