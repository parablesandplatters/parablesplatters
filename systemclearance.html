Parables & Platters - Legal Operating License

Parables & Platters is a legally registered business operating under the South African constitution. Our company is officially registered with the CIPC (Enterprise Number: 2025/094299/07) and holds a valid tax number (9324863258).

We offer high-quality meals, including pap, steak, and wors, served to the working-class community. Our services include delivery via various platforms such as our website, WhatsApp, phone calls, social media, and email. We also operate as a mobile kitchen where customers can purchase directly from our car, offering convenience for those who cannot place an order in advance.

All transactions are currently cash-based as our payment gateways are in the process of deployment. We operate in Umhlanga, Hillcrest, and Westville, and we pride ourselves on the unique and high-quality products we offer.

As a legal business, we should not face prejudice or discrimination while carrying out our operations, whether on the streets or at our various locations.

We value transparency, respect, and the rights of our customers and aim to contribute positively to the community we serve.
