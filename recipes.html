Pap

  • 01. 1L water into Pot

  • 02. Allow to boil

  • 03. In a separate bowl, mix maize meal with 2 cups of water.

  • 04. Gradually stir the maize meal mixture into the boiling water.

  • 05. Stir continuously with wisk to avoid lumps. Be patient!.

  • 06. Let it cook on medium heat for 10 minutes.

  • 07. Adjust thickness by adding more maize meal as needed. Cook for an additional 5 minutes for perfect consistency.

  • 08. Pinch of Salt.

  • 09. Enjoy your delicious pap!

Braai Wors

  • start your fire by putting the charcoal on top of the grill and then putting paper, cardboard and plastic or just paper alone - anything you find, and then put these underneath with your grill of charcoal on top, and then light it up. the charcoal will burn perfectly from underneath.

  • once you see your charcoal being red, load it inside the stand carefully.

  • clean your grill with half an onion.

  • let it heat up some more and then once you got red hot flaming charcoal all over, you would have already marinaded your wors with spices of your choosing, and then place it on your grill to cook.

  • With wors, we always want to limit flames to avoid the skin burning black, so always have your cup of water on standby to sprinkle.

  • as your wors is braaing, do some small cuts to it to allow to cook thoroughly on the inside. keep turning it, and then once you're satisfied, you're all done with Wors.
