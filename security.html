Security Measures - Parables & Platters

Our Commitment to Security & Safety

At Parables & Platters, safety is our priority. We recognize the importance of protecting our team, our partners, and the communities we serve. Together, we work hand-in-hand with the South African Police Service and the Hawks to ensure a safe environment for everyone in our areas of operation.

Our Security Measures

1. Real-Time Communication with Local Authorities

We maintain a direct line of communication with the South African Police Service and the Hawks for emergency responses and proactive safety measures. Our operatives are trained to contact authorities instantly should any threats arise, ensuring prompt action.

2. Secure Location Tracking

Each vehicle and staff member is equipped with state-of-the-art GPS tracking to ensure real-time location sharing. In case of any emergency, the police or Hawks can trace the vehicle's movements to provide immediate assistance.

3. 24/7 Surveillance

Our team operates under constant surveillance with high-tech cameras placed at strategic locations, ensuring that our movements and actions are constantly monitored for our safety. Any abnormal activity is instantly reported.

4. Armed Security Escort

When deemed necessary, we engage armed escorts from local authorities to accompany our delivery vehicles to ensure the utmost protection of both our staff and goods.

5. Psychological Safety Measures

We also focus on the mental well-being of our team. Regular counseling and safety awareness programs are conducted to ensure that our team is always prepared and mentally strong in any situation.
